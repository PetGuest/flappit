Frequently asked questions

What do I need to buy?

Nothing, as long as your TV is a Smart TV with a browser. You just open Flappit on the screen and link it to your account. If the TV is older, you can use a ready-configured HDMI stick.

Who manages it day to day?

Anyone on your team. From reception they can write messages, change content or schedule a sequence from a phone or computer.

Do I need a technician?

No. Flappit is designed so any hotel or business team can set it up with no technical knowledge.

What happens if the internet goes down?

The screen keeps the last saved content and reconnects automatically when the connection returns. It never goes black.

Can I use my own logo and colours?

Yes. You can customise the brand strip, the board colours, the logo and other visual elements so it matches your business identity.

Does it work on vertical screens?

Yes. Flappit works on both horizontal and vertical screens, such as lobby TVs or reception totems.

Is there a minimum contract?

No. The subscription is monthly, the first 7 days are free, and you can cancel whenever you like in 2 clicks.
