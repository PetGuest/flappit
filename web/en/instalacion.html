Installation questions

Do I need to buy any gadget?

No, if your TV has an internet browser. If it's old or doesn't have one, you'll need an HDMI stick (Fire TV, Android TV or Chromecast, ~€30). We can send it to you already set up with your account: you plug it in behind the TV and your board shows up on its own.

My Samsung/LG doesn't have the browser app. What do I do?

Some recent smart TVs no longer come with a browser. The reliable route in that case is the HDMI stick: it launches on its own, opens Flappit in full screen and stays fixed. It's the option we recommend for installing without surprises.

Does it work without internet?

You need wifi or a cable to receive content and for time-based scheduling. But once the content is on the screen, it stays even if the connection drops. No black screens.

How many screens can I set up?

As many as you like. Each screen has its own 4-letter code and is managed from the same dashboard. They can show the same thing at once or different content in each area.

Can I update the messages from my phone?

Yes, from anywhere. The dashboard is fully mobile: change tonight's special from the kitchen or push out an alert from the other side of the world.

How long does it take to get set up?

A few minutes. Put Flappit on the screen, type the 4 letters and your first message: done. Exploring the scheduling and advanced options takes a little longer, but the first board really is quick.

What if I get stuck?

Just write to us and we'll sort it out, almost always in a single email. Most questions are solved in minutes.
